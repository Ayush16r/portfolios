About SOA ITER

SOA ITER is a student-led initiative that aims to foster innovation and collaboration among students. Our mission is to create a platform where students can share ideas, work on projects, and develop skills that will prepare them for the future.

We believe in the power of community and the potential of every student to contribute to the world of technology and innovation.
